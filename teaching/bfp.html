For the academic course 24/25, I’m supervising a number of TFGs. Due to the amount of work for this academic course, I’ve no plans for supervising more TFGs in the 24/25 academic course

For the academic course 25/26, due to the high demand, I’ll recommend you to check as soon as possible the availability of tutors.

The Bachelor’s Final Project (tfg) is a project that all the students have to do in order to finish the degree in Computer Science.

I’ll be glad to hear your ideas, but keep in mind that my main interest is related with my doctoral thesis: Responsive Web Interaction. Usually we work on topics heavily related with the web and how users interact in innovative ways, e.g. offline environments, multi-device/screen interaction, newest technologies …, and in many contexts, such as health or education, to name a few.

In the meanwhile, here are some projects I’m interested on:

  • Web Interaction Hub
  • Técnicas de Visualización para el Soporte de la Interacción en Plataformas Web Offline/Progresivas

I’ve developed a template (Template for the Bachelor’s Final Project (TFG)) which can be used to write the TFG. Feel free to use it.

Feel free to Contact me if you want to work on my topics or if you want to talk about your interests.
